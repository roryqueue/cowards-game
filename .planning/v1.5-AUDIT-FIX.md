# v1.5 Audit Fix

## Audit Result

No remaining findings after the final audit pass.

## Findings Fixed During Audit

- Recall Hunter initially produced a degenerate 7-0 tournament sweep. It was tuned away from a direct Backstab Hunter clone, source hashes changed, and the demo evidence was regenerated.
- The first accepted Advanced set still behaved too much like annotated Starter derivatives. The second-take retune replaced the thin wrappers with a shared tactical baseline for all Advanced seeds: leave starting edge early, seek next-move Backstab opportunities, avoid contraction-edge risk when possible, avoid direct self-stoning, avoid off-board/wall/stone/terrain/immediate-reversal moves, and apply archetype-specific bias only after those fundamentals.
- The second-take evidence was regenerated. Final standings were non-degenerate: Center Gravity 6-1-0, Stonewall Shear 5-2-0, Rear Guard Sentinel 5-2-0, Vanguard Pressure 4-3-0.
- Evidence sanity checks showed 33 replay-backed Chronicles, 7103 `MOVE_ADVANCED`, 412 `BACKSTAB_RESOLVED`, 0 `MOVE_BLOCKED`, 0 direct `TURN_TO_STONE`, and 0 `MOVED_OFF_BOARD`. The remaining 17 `NO_ADVANCE` stones are engine cleanup for trapped/late selected Soldiers, not explicit Strategy self-stoning.
- The v1.5 demo script initially checked the wrong public DTO counted-status location. The script now checks `metadata.countedStatus` and explicitly marks complete replay-backed local demo MatchSets counted.
- The initial v1.5 tournament workload was too heavy for local verification. The final demo uses one deterministic 8-entrant smoke round robin plus five example MatchSets, with non-durable local-evidence framing.
- The in-process Workshop smoke gauntlet tests are intentionally long-running because they simulate deterministic Matches synchronously. Their per-test timeouts were raised to match the current v1.4/v1.5 gauntlet cost without loosening assertions.
