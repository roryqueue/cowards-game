# Phase 164 Plan

## Goal

Define the v1.23 floor, hostile Strategy threat model, and permitted v1.24 claims before abuse probes begin.

## Scope

In scope:
- Summarize v1.23 proof, promotion, ABI, privacy, no-fallback, and signed-in evidence as the v1.24 baseline.
- Document hostile Strategy assumptions across JS/TS, Python, Rust, Zig, and WASM/WASI lanes.
- Create a claims contract that separates readiness evidence, counted eligibility, non-counted exhibition beta, and production sandbox certification.
- Preserve Go/runtime-service ownership and public-output privacy exclusions.

Out of scope:
- Runtime promotion, non-JS counted play, production sandbox certification, ABI migration, or source-code implementation.

## Tasks

1. Read current milestone context and archived v1.23 evidence.
2. Produce a baseline artifact summarizing v1.23 decisions and evidence gaps.
3. Produce a hostile Strategy threat model covering capability, resource, ABI, artifact, unavailable-lane, privacy, and fallback risks.
4. Produce a claims contract that explicitly keeps JS/TS counted and Python/Rust/Zig non-counted exhibition beta.
5. Add a checkable public-safe Markdown summary for operators.
