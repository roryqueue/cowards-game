# Phase 20 Plan: Trial Ladder Season Model

## Scope

1. Add trial ladder season and entry persistence.
2. Enforce one active Strategy Revision per user per season.
3. Lock entry snapshots with revision id, owner, handle, source hash, runtime, engine compatibility, and status.
4. Add public season DTOs with resettable standings policy and no permanent ratings.
5. Add routes/server methods for create, enter, withdraw, and view.

## Acceptance

Season statuses and policies are visible, entries are immutable snapshots, and exhibition self-play remains unchanged.
