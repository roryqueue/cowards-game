**Date:** 2026-05-19
**Phase:** 22-Public Profiles and Strategy Cards
**Mode:** Abbreviated per user request after Phase 21.

## Recommended Decisions Accepted

- Public profiles are handle-first competition identity pages.
- Public profiles show Strategies, ladder history, record, MatchSet links, replay links, and public activity.
- Public Strategy cards show safe metadata, lineage, hashes, compatibility, history, records, results, and replays.
- Strategy source and private runtime/debug data remain hidden.
- Owner affordances are server-authorized and secondary.
- Public DTO leak assertions are required.
- Pages stay dense, scannable, and competition-oriented.

## Alternatives Not Pursued

- Social/community features such as follows, comments, likes, galleries, badges, and messaging.
- Public source previews.
- Rich search/discovery.
- Marketing-style profile pages.

## Deferred Ideas

- Community/social surfaces.
- Standalone public starter gallery.
- Public Strategy source sharing.
