# Phase 15 Plan 01: Competition Contracts and Entrant Snapshots

**Status:** Planned
**Requirements:** COMP-01, COMP-02, COMP-03, COMP-04, COMP-05, COMP-08

## Goal

Define serializable competition presets, entrant snapshots, stale revision behavior, and publication policy.

## Tasks

1. Add Smoke Exhibition and Standard Exhibition preset contracts.
2. Add entrant snapshot contracts with revision id/hash, owner id, handle, label, runtime/engine compatibility, and snapshot time.
3. Persist locked entrant snapshots for MatchSets.
4. Document stale revision behavior through tests.

## Acceptance

- Locked snapshots remain stable after newer revisions exist.
- Public snapshot data excludes Strategy source and memory.
