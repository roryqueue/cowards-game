# Phase 172 Plan

## Goal

Prove JS/TS counted support and Python/Rust/Zig non-counted exhibition beta regressions still work with public-safe result and replay evidence.

## Scope

In scope:
- Signed-in proof for JS/TS counted Strategy support.
- Signed-in proof for Python, Rust, and Zig non-counted exhibition beta support.
- MatchSet result pages with public-safe runtime/evidence labels.
- Replay pages with plausible full Match starts, visible Soldiers and terrain inside declared board bounds, and nonblank replay boards.
- Public privacy scans and no silent fallback evidence.

Out of scope:
- Promoting Python, Rust, Zig, WASM/WASI, direct exports, or WIT to counted play.
- Owner-private source/memory/objective exposure.

## Tasks

1. Reuse the v1.23 signed-in multi-compiler proof style for v1.24.
2. Create or update proof coverage for JS/TS, Python, Rust, and Zig lanes.
3. Open MatchSet result pages and verify public-safe labels.
4. Open replay pages and verify board plausibility, in-bounds pieces, terrain, and nonblank rendering.
5. Scan public output for private markers and raw diagnostics.
6. Record no-fallback behavior for unavailable, stopped, stale, mismatched, malformed, and capability-invalid lanes.
7. Generate signed-in proof JSON and Markdown artifacts.
