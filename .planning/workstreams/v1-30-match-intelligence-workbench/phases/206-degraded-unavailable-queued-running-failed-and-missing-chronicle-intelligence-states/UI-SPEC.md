# Phase 206 UI Spec: Degraded and Unavailable Intelligence States

## Intent

Non-happy states should be useful and honest without showing fake tactical analysis.

## Requirements

- Queued/running copy says intelligence is pending.
- Missing/no-result replay pages do not show intelligence panels.
- Result pages show state-specific limits for each fixture.
- Public copy avoids private field names and internal diagnostics.

## Acceptance

- v1.30 proof renders every frozen result fixture and unavailable replay state.
