# Plan 09-05: Integrated Validation Gate and Provable Board Contradictions

## Objective

Wire the grammar and snapshot validators into the public validation path, then reject provably impossible board transitions without building a second engine.

Purpose: This completes GRAM-01 through GRAM-05 and GRAM-08 while honoring D-01 through D-04 and D-10/D-11. It keeps validation bounded to Chronicle event/snapshot contradictions and preserves replay reconstruction as the state comparison tool.

Output: `validateChronicle` as the strict acceptance gate for replay loading, plus focused negative fixtures and legality tests.
