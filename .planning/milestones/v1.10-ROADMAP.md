## Current Milestone: v1.10 Service Boundary Completion and Go Read-Model Decision

**Status:** In Progress
**Phases:** 64-69
**Granularity:** Standard
**Requirements:** 29/29 mapped

## Overview

v1.10 continues the v1.9 service-boundary ownership move. It starts by re-baselining the remaining 34 broad web report-only direct persistence/runtime offenses, migrates only read/user surfaces that can be proven behind `@cowards/service`, and keeps writes, Strategy source, Workshop execution, replay owner-debug assembly, production sandbox promotion, and counted non-JS play out of scope. The single approved Go read-model expansion is public `GET /public/strategies/{strategyId}`, implemented only with generated TypeScript-service-backed parity fixtures, GET-only routing, topology checks, privacy checks, and rollback criteria.
