## Product-Learning Note

The tuned seeded demo now produces more credible competition and matches that commonly last into contraction. Escape Artist and Ring Runner remain weaker than the top center/pressure doctrines, which is acceptable for exposing varied approaches, but future starter balance work should keep checking that weaker doctrines still teach useful counterplay rather than looking broken.
