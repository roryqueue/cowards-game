# Milestone v1.5: Strategy Workshop Power Tools and Advanced Strategy Library

**Status:** SHIPPED 2026-05-21

## Overview

v1.5 improved the Strategy Workshop from a single-test authoring surface into a deterministic Strategy lab, then used v1.4 result evidence to create, tune, validate, and publish a distinct Advanced Strategy Library. The milestone generated replay-backed example MatchSets and a completed local advanced-only demo tournament with public-safe Strategy cards, player/profile pages, MatchSet pages, and representative replays.

## Phases

**Requirements:** WSHOP-01 through WSHOP-09, DIAG-01 through DIAG-06
**Status:** Complete

**Goal:** Analyze v1.4 results and define reusable evidence summaries, Chronicle-derived behavior signals, dominance review triggers, and local report shape.
