## Future Requirements

- Future public result/replay contract expansion requires a separate app contract version or explicitly proven backward-compatible addition.

- Future replacement Match or MatchSet provenance requires a separate public scoring and replay-link design.
- Future richer replay explanation that needs StrategyMemory, SoldierMemory, objective payloads, or owner debug data must remain owner-private and must not become public output by default.
